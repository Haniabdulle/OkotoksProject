Welcome!

Mikado Sushi & Robata aims to provide the freshest and best sushi and sashimi. Our genuine Robata grills, imported from Japan, allow for very high temperatures while keeping meats, seafood and vegetables juicy and tender. With three locations around Edmonton, we encourage you to make reservations for your next visit, or order your Mikado for pickup.
